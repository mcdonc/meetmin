## Summary

The group discussed the emergence and characteristics of Glam Rock in the 1970s. The conversation covered the visual and musical aspects of the movement, highlighting key artists like David Bowie, T. Rex, and Roxy Music. The discussion also contrasted the "art-school" British glam scene (Bowie, Ferry, Eno) with the "street-level" American glam scene (New York Dolls, Alice Cooper). The group explored the lineage of glam, noting its influence on punk, new wave, and 80s hair metal.
