# Led Zeppelin

A seminal rock band of the 1970s known for their massive influence on hard rock and heavy metal.

## Mentions

- [2026-07-15](../../minutes/2026-07-15-70s-rock-discussion.md): Cited as a primary influence for modern bands like Foo Fighters and as a source of highly sampled drum breaks (e.g., "When the Levee Breaks").
