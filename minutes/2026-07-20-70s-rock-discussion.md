## Summary
The final session of the 20-day discussion series focused on the personal significance of 1970s music. Participants shared diverse perspectives: Dana highlighted the era's musical ambition and the "permission to be ambitious" found in prog rock; Rick emphasized the cultural weight and consequence of the music; Sheila discussed the radical DIY ethos of punk; and Marcus noted the decade's inherent creative contradictions. The group also reflected on the vastness of the era, noting many significant omissions (Stevie Wonder, Bob Marley, Krautrock, Jazz Fusion, etc.), and discussed potential future directions for the group.
