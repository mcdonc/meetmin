# Lynyrd Skynyrd

Lynyrd Skynyrd was a prominent Southern rock band known for a more compact, direct, and arranged sound compared to the improvisational style of the Allman Brothers.

## Mentions
- [2026-07-05](../../minutes/2026-07-05-70s-rock-discussion.md): Cited as a contrast to the Allman Brothers; noted for their three-guitar arrangements and the significance of the song "Free Bird." The band's history was marked by the tragic 1977 plane crash.
