## Summary
The group discussed "guilty pleasure" songs and albums from the 1970s—tracks that are highly enjoyable but often dismissed by critics or excluded from "greatest of all time" lists. The discussion touched on various genres including arena rock, orchestral pop, operatic rock, and prog-adjacent radio hits.

## Decisions
- The group agreed that "Bohemian Rhapsody" by Queen is a legitimate masterpiece and does not qualify as a "guilty pleasure."
