## Summary
The group discussed the importance of rhythm sections (drums and bass) in 1970s rock. Key themes included the "human feel" and "groove" of the era compared to modern quantized production. The discussion covered legendary drummers like John Bonham, Bill Bruford, Neil Peart, and Keith Moon, as well as iconic bassists such as Chris Squire, John Entwistle, Geddy Lee, Geezer Butler, John Paul Jones, Tina Weymouth, and Phil Lynott.
