## Summary
The group discussed the evolution of the live music experience during the 1970s, tracing the transition from intimate club shows and large outdoor festivals to theatrical arena performances and massive stadium spectacles. Key themes included the loss of intimacy as venues scaled up, the rise of bootleg culture, and the shift from raw, communal outdoor gatherings to highly produced, immersive indoor shows.
