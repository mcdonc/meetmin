# Muscle Shoals Sound Studio
A famous studio in Alabama known for its "Southern feel," featuring a rhythm section (The Swampers) that backed numerous major rock artists.

## Mentions
- [2026-07-14](../../minutes/2026-07-14-70s-rock-discussion.md): Discussed as a key American studio that influenced the sound of artists like the Rolling Stones and Lynyrd Skynyrd.
