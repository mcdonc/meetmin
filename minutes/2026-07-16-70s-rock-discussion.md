## Summary
The group discussed various "underrated and forgotten" bands from the 1970s, exploring why certain artists failed to achieve mainstream success despite their talent. Themes included industry mismanagement, the impact of sexism on the rock press, and the overshadowing of earlier pioneers by more "flashy" successors.
