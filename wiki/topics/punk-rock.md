# Punk Rock

A musical movement and subculture that emerged in the late 1970s, characterized by a DIY ethic and significant influence on subsequent genres and fashion.

## Mentions

- [2026-07-15](../../minutes/2026-07-15-70s-rock-discussion.md): Discussed as the progenitor of hardcore, pop-punk, emo, and indie. Its DIY infrastructure (independent labels, fanzines) transformed the music industry.
- [2026-07-15](../../minutes/2026-07-15-70s-rock-discussion.md): Its fashion (safety pins, ripped clothes, combat boots) continues to cycle through modern aesthetics.
- [2026-07-20](../../minutes/2026-07-20-70s-rock-discussion.md): Discussed as a source of radical possibility and the idea that art belongs to everyone.
