# David Bowie

A highly influential artist known for his constant evolution, persona changes, and prolific output during the 1970s.

## Mentions
- [2026-07-03](../../minutes/2026-07-03-70s-rock-discussion.md): Discussed for his songwriting genius on *Hunky Dory* and his rapid-fire album releases between 1971 and 1976.
- [2026-07-06](../../minutes/2026-07-06-70s-rock-discussion.md): Cited as having perfected glam rock through concept pieces like *The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars*, using persona as part of the art.
