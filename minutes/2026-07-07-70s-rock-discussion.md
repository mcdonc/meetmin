## Summary
The group discussed the "golden age" of rock guitar in the 1970s, highlighting various virtuosos across different subgenres including hard rock, prog rock, blues-rock, and Latin rock. The discussion emphasized that 70s guitar greatness was defined by unique "voices" and distinct tones rather than just technical speed. The group also addressed the historical oversight of female guitarists in the "guitar hero" narrative.
