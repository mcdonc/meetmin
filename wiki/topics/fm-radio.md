# FM Radio

A radio format that experienced a "golden age" in the 1970s, characterized by DJ autonomy and the ability to play deep album cuts, which helped build audiences for progressive rock bands.

- [2026-07-10](../../minutes/2026-07-10-70s-rock-discussion.md): Discussed as the "discovery engine" for 70s rock, before becoming more corporate and formatted in the 1980s.
